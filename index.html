Instructions

This is not a clinical, diagnostic, or treatment evaluation. It is a usability and safety spot-check of English rewritten texts.

  • Meaning preserved: 1 = serious meaning/task change, 3 = mostly preserved with concerns, 5 = fully preserves meaning and task requirements.
  • Dyslexia-friendly presentation: 1 = not helpful or harder, 3 = somewhat clearer, 5 = clearly reduces reading burden.
  • Decision: Accept = usable as is, Revise = needs human edits, Reject = unsuitable or unsafe.
  • Focus line: use the reading-mask controls above each text box to view one line at a time; the surrounding text is covered by a 70% black overlay.
